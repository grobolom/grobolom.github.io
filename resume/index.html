About Me

I am particularly well versed in working with legacy code and updating existing systems to cover them in unit tests and modernize them in preparation for adding significant new features. I write down a lot of my thoughts on this learning (an some off-topic material as well) on my blog.

I'm looking for a company that has devs that are as interested in learning and getting better as I am. Software development is more than my career - I love coding, and I want to be the best that I can at it. I want to learn from others that are better than me and grow along with them. I also want to work on projects that can really help people, and I think working with wonderful people is the first step towards that goal.
