About Me

I'm a software dev with a bit over six years of coding experience, primarily in the PHP/PostgreSQL stack on the backend and the usual frontend HTML/JS/CSS. I've worked with Symfony and Slim for PHP and have experimented considerably with Angular 1.x and React.

I am particularly well versed in working with legacy code and updating existing systems to cover them in unit tests and modernize them in preparation for adding significant new features. I believe strongly in testable code, and practice test driven development as often as I can. I'm also a strong believer in SOLID design principles, though I find these tend to come out naturally when practicing TDD.

However, the skills I am most confident in are my ability to work well with others and my focus on a lifelong pursuit of learning. I read every programming book that I think is worth my time (espcially Sandi Metz's POODR and the recent GOOSgbt). I'm constantly listening to coding podcasts (Ruby Rogues, Developer Tea, Destroy All Software) and experimenting with the latest technologies such as React. I write down a lot of my thoughts on this learning (an some off-topic material as well) on my blog.

I'm looking for a company that has devs that are as interested in learning and getting better as I am. Software development is more than my career - I love coding, and I want to be the best that I can at it. I want to learn from others that are better than me and grow along with them. I also want to work on projects that can really help people, and I think working with wonderful people is the first step towards that goal.
