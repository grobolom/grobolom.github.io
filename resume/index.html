Simple Food Log

A project currently in progress, the Simple Food Log (SFL) arose out of my frustrations with existing diet and logging apps. Many popular applications have too many features (MyFitnessPal) which make entering your meals slow; this results in more difficult dieting, as users must take much more time and mental focus to keep recording their caloric intake. Manual solutions such as Excel or Google Sheets are effective but a hassle to maintain; charts need to be updated when they run out of bounds, new workbooks have to be started or sections copied-and-pasted to continue logging.

The major design goals of SFL are: speed of food entry; speed of calorie logging; easy averaging and persistent diet tracking; automatic charting of progress. The intent is to marry the simplicity of manual calorie tracking with the convencience and positive reinforcement provided by quick charting and consistent meal tracking.
